Our Mission

Predicting Resilience

At Logicore, we believe that data is the strongest levee against natural disasters. Traditional flood warnings often come too late; our vision is to transform reactive emergency response into proactive climate intelligence.

By leveraging advanced Random Forest Regressors and real-time satellite telemetry, FloodGuard AI doesn't just watch the rain—it understands the landscape. We analyze the critical intersection of precipitation intensity, soil saturation, and local elevation to provide high-fidelity risk assessments before the first drop hits the ground.

Our goal is to democratize disaster-tech, providing local authorities and citizens with the precise lead time necessary to secure lives, property, and the future of our communities.

The Future: Recursive Learning

Real-time Adaptation

Moving beyond static datasets, our future iterations will use Recursive Learning to ingest current weather reports and news cycles instantly.
